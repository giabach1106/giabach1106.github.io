Pandemic

in daily — Jun 16, 2022

Hanoi during social quanratine,
Photo by VnExpress.

I just finished my 10th grade year, and have been enjoying summer vacation for a few days.

How do I feel about the past school year? Bored!

Most of the year we had to study online, because of the social distancing.
So my daily life would look like this: “Wake up at 7:29, log in to Zoom class at exactly 7:30, take attendance and… go back to sleep.”
Of course not every day is like that, but I’m really fed up with Online learning, no communication, no sunlight, and especially, no exercise.

However, this quarantine made me learn some new things that I had never thought of before.
That’s Cooking
That’s right, living 16 years of my life, and I’ve never cooked for myself.
And today, I successfully made “Lolot leaf rolls”

Simply includes a little pork, chicken eggs, fresh betel leaves, purple onions, common spices, etc. Even though it’s a bit salty, from now on everyone in the family will have to look at me in a different light. hehehe.

In addition, I also taught myself to play the guitar.
It’s my dad’s old Acoustic
Actually, I had to put a lot of effort into changing the strings, as well as re-tuning each note
But, eventually it works just perfectly.

That’s all, even though it’s a bit senseless, it feels like I’ve achieved some kind of cool achievement. This epidemic has made me realize many things and have the opportunity to spend more time with myself and everyone in my family.
So, thank you Covid-19, I guessed.
